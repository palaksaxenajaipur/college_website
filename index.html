Welcome to KVGIT

The Khandelwal Vaish Girls Institute of Technology (KVGIT) is a distinguished institution affiliated with the University of Rajasthan (UOR) and Rajasthan Technical University (RTU). Approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and the Government of Rajasthan, KVGIT is centrally located in the prestigious Vaishali Nagar. This esteemed technical and professional college, exclusively for girls, is dedicated to societal advancement by the Shri Khandelwal Vaishya Educational Trust. KVGIT is a leading undergraduate institution for liberal learning in Rajasthan, committed to advancing knowledge, fostering critical reflection, and promoting rigorous discussion and uninhibited discourse. The college embraces innovative educational practices to enhance creative learning and enrich civic life. The KVGIT campus spans approximately 10,000 square yards with a carpet area exceeding 120,000 square feet, featuring two large building blocks designed to meet students’ diverse needs. The peaceful, eco-friendly, and earthquake-resistant campus includes an intelligent building management system conforming to international standards. The aesthetically designed infrastructure encompasses an administrative block, classrooms, laboratories, a library, faculty rooms, a centralized air-cooled hostel, an auditorium, and various amenities.
