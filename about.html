Who We Are

KVGIT is dedicated to supporting financially disadvantaged and meritorious students through various schemes, promoting inclusivity and innovation by drawing on diverse perspectives and viewpoints. The college offers scholarships and paid internships, providing extensive access and financial support to first-generation, rural students, and those who serve their country and communities. Located in Jaipur, KVGIT offers students numerous opportunities to engage with the cityâ€™s cultural institutions, seamlessly integrating on-campus learning with real-world experiences. In addition to degree programs, KVGIT offers a variety of affordable short-term courses in CPBFI, NPTEL, Automotive Sales, Digital Business Skills, and Yoga, equipping students with a competitive edge in the modern workplace. KVGIT aims to instill values that help students grow into responsible citizens. The various departmental societies extend beyond the university curriculum, organizing lectures, seminars, workshops, cultural programs, excursions, and more. Participation in annual events such as Annual Day, Sports Day, Scouts & Guide, and NSS Day is mandatory for all students. Additionally, students are encouraged to participate in inter-college and other sports functions for holistic development. The college features vibrant clubs, including Abhivyakti, Avbodhan, Anandam, Samsidhi, Srijan, Prakriti, and Techno-Club, offering a wide range of extracurricular activities.
